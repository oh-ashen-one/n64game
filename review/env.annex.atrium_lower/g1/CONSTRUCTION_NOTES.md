# Meridian Annex simulation-threshold construction notes

## Evidence purpose

`ART_TARGET.png` is a 1600×1200 original-IP visual target for the retained simulation-threshold corner. It is not a shippable texture, final gameplay capture, Gate pass, or authorization to copy image-space lighting into baked textures. `CONCEPT_RENDER.png` remains the editable Blender construction evidence. The two views must be read together: the target defines finish and composition; the Blender source owns measurable geometry and topology.

The skylight spine, monitor console, and four-pad dais visible in the target remain separate production owners. Their appearance here establishes scene composition only and does not transfer model, material, or output ownership into `env.annex.atrium_lower`.

## Locked composition

- Preserve the central warm portal as the primary route cue and keep its full opening readable at 320×240.
- Preserve two open actor pads and a clear player route between foreground, pads, and portal.
- Use the skylight spine as the cool top-light source and strongest longitudinal landmark.
- Place one monitor console near the entry edge where it reads as a calibration station without narrowing the walkable route.
- Treat the four-pad dais as a deliberate floor landmark; it must remain flush enough to avoid collision traps.
- Keep the two wall service cages asymmetric and functional: one stores maintenance equipment, the other supports simulation machinery.

## Production rebuild targets

- Replace broad empty wall fields with modular ceramic panels, recessed service bays, rails, vents, conduits, and recent-activity dressing while preserving collision readability.
- Reserve geometry for silhouette changes and route landmarks. Use authored vertex-color gradients, a shared ceramic/metal trim atlas, and small state textures instead of unique high-resolution materials.
- Add at least five purposeful, non-filler props in the benchmark view: monitor console, four-pad dais, cable run, maintenance tray or tool cluster, and one service container or vent assembly.
- Use warm amber practicals at the portal and work surfaces, cool cyan skylight spill, cobalt occlusion, and only one restrained violet instrument state.
- Keep signs symbolic. Do not bake generated pseudo-text or image noise into production textures.
- Validate the final sector at the benchmark gameplay camera and native 320×240 before any G1 decision; a beauty render alone is insufficient.

## Feasibility and ownership guardrails

- The environment subset remains bounded to `sector.atrium_lower.sim_threshold_corner`, approximately 1,600–2,200 visible triangles and no more than 72 KiB of working textures.
- Instanced landmark and prop packages retain their own source/output manifests and seven-gate reviews.
- The generated target may guide composition, palette, material breakup, and dressing density only. Every production mesh, UV, texture, collision surface, light choice, and camera placement must be deliberately authored and reviewed.
- G1 remains `IN_PROGRESS`. No topology, conversion, runtime, performance, or final-quality claim is made by this packet.
